Importance of traditional food


Indian cuisine includes a variety of spices, where every spice has many health benefits. Including a variety of spices and vegetables in your diet is important for living healthy. Eat local and seasonal vegetables and fruits at the right time for good health and strong immunity.

Traditional foods are nutrient-rich and have a long history of supporting health and wellness. Also, traditional ingredients and raw materials actually can boost the health, medicinal, and nutraceutical values of traditional foods

The idea here is to tell that Indian food has always been nourishing, wholesome and healthy. Cook your food with natural and traditional ingredients and there is probably no food as healthy as Indian food.
