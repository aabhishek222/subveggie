Return & Refund Policy

Thanks for purchasing our products

Below are the conditions when you can be eligible for the return and refund policy

  1. If the delivered package is not sealed (please do not accept the delivery in this case)
  2. If the delivered food item is rotten or not in consumable state
  3. If you have not received the ordered product but instead received some other item

In case of point 1, you should deny the delivery to the delivery personnel and contact us to let us know the issue faced by you. We will help your refund to be processed.

We request you to always record a video while opening the received package showing clearly the package and the inside food item. This will help you and us with better customer satisfaction.

In case if you face an issue as mentioned in point 2 and 3 above, we request you to send the recorded video clip on our customer service details (email support@subveggie.com and whatsapp at 8112216151). The video should be sent within 1 day of the package delivery, in case of further delay we may not be able to assist you since food items are perishable items. While sending the whatsapp or email to our customer support, clearly mention your sub veggie profile details and order id of the ordered item. This will help us identify your order and serve you better.

Our team will inspect the same and if it a genuine issue is identified we will help you to get your refund processed within next 4 to 5 working days.

If anything is unclear or you have more questions feel free to contact our customer support team.
